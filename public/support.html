FAQs

How to book a taxi?
Navigate to the "Home" screen, select "Taxi Services", choose your pickup and drop-off locations, pick your preferred vehicle type, and confirm the booking. You will be matched with a pilot instantly.
Cancellation policy
You can cancel your bookings for free up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellations made within 2 hours may incur a small cancellation fee depending on the service.
Payment methods
We support direct email booking request inquiries. All reservations are coordinated and finalized offline with the service providers.
Loyalty rewards
Our loyalty program is currently being upgraded. In the future, you will earn reward points for every booking which can be redeemed for exclusive discounts.

24/7 Concierge

Our premium concierge service is available around the clock to ensure your Yercaud stay is perfect. Contact us for special requests.
